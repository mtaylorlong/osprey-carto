This map shows every sighting of Ospreys (fish-eating raptors found world-wide) submitted to the eBird database in 2013. The map is animated by the date the sighting was made, revealing the ebb and flow of these birds' range as they move from their warmer wintering grounds to their more northern summer grounds. An osprey may travel more than 160,000 migration miles during its 15-to-20-year lifetime (allaboutbirds.org).
